How we work

  1. 1

    Discovery

    We align on goals, constraints, stakeholders, and success criteria so scope stays realistic.

  2. 2

    Proposal

    You get a clear plan: deliverables, milestones, communication rhythm, and a fixed or phased estimate.

  3. 3

    Build

    We execute in tight loops—CAD, UI, or visuals—with incremental reviews so feedback lands early.

  4. 4

    Review

    Structured checkpoints against acceptance criteria keep quality and timelines predictable.

  5. 5

    Handoff

    You receive assets, documentation, and optional support so the work stays useful after launch.
